supplementary crystallographic information comment indolizines , the nitrogen containing heterocyclic systems , are widely distributed in nature ; in particular , indolizine derivatives are an important class of heterocyclic bioactive compounds with a wide range of applications , such as pharmaceutical drugs , potential central nervous system depressants , calcium entry blockers , cardiovascular agents , spectral sensitizers and novel dyes ( gubin et al. , 1992 ; gupta et al. , 2003 ; poty et al. , 1994 ; hema et al. , 2003 ). moreover indolizine derivatives have been found to possess a variety of biological activities such as antiinflammatory ( malonne et al. , 1998 ) , antiviral ( medda et al. , 2003 ) .
