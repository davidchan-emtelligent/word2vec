the dihedral angles between the benzo - thia - zole ring systems and the phenol rings are 4.1 ( 4 ) and 5.8 ( 4 ) degrees , indicating an almost planar geometry .
weak intra - and inter - molecular c - - h ... o hydrogen bonds are observed .
in the crystal , weak pi - pi inter - actions between aromatic and thia - zole rings [ centroid - centroid distances = 3.626 ( 3 ) and 3.873 ( 3 ) a ] link the mol - ecules into a two - dimensional supra - molecular network along the bc plane .
