sinus histiocytosis with massive lymphadenopathy ( shlm ) disease is considered to be an indolent and self - limiting pathology .
however , severe morbidity and mortality have been attributed to complications of shlm .
lower respiratory tract involvement is rarely reported and is often unfavorable , and carries a particularly grave prognosis .
a case of shlm is reported , in which the patient had lower respiratory and pleural involvement .
