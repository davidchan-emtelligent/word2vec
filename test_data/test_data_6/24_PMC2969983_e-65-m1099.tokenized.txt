however , nitrite coordinated diethylenediamine chelated co ( iii ) complexes have not been reported .
in this work , a new diethylenediamine chelated co ( iii ) complexes coordinated by a nitrite have been synthesized , and its structure is reported here .
the structure of the cation is given in fig. 1. there are two crystallographically independent molecules in the asymmetric unit .
the two molecules are almost identical .
the cation consists of cobalt ( iii ) coordinated by four nitrogen atoms from two ethylenediamine and two oxygen atoms from one nitrite .
there is also a hydroxide and a nitrite appeared as the counter ions in the crystal .
the co - - n and co - - o distances are normal ( sharma et al. , 2005 , bruggemann et al. , 1994 ) .
in the crystal structure of title compound , there is strongly multipoint directional hydrogen bonds interactions among the [ co ( en ) 2 ( no3 )] 2 + subunit , hydroxide and nitrite anions in the range of 0.278 ( 3 ) nm - 0.3424 ( 12 ) nm. thus , the complex cations and the hydroxide and nitrite anions are connected to result in a three - dimensional supramolecular framework through o - h ... o and n - h ... o hydrogen - bonding interactions .
