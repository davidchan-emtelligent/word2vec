supplementary crystallographic information comment 5 - acyloxypyrazoles have received considerable attention due to their high antibacterial and biological activities ( bai et al. , 2002 , varma , 1999 ) .
as part of our ongoing search for biologically active molecules , the title compound was synthesized and characterized by x - ray diffraction ( fig. 1 ) .
the three phenyl rings ( c1 - - c6 , r.m.s. deviation = 0.0047 a ; c12 - - c17 , r.m.s. deviation = 0.0061 a ; c19 - - c24 , r.m.s. deviation = 0.0045 a ) are twisted with respect to the central pyrazole ring ( n1,2 / c8 - - c10 , r.m.s. deviation = 0.0065 a ) making dihedral angles of 71.56 ( 9 ) degrees , 57.55 ( 8 ) degrees and 39.33 ( 1 ) degrees , respectively .
as expected , there are no classic hydrogen bonds in the structure .
experimental 1.74 g of 1 - phenyl - 3 - methyl - 5 - pyrazolone was dissolved in 10 ml dioxane by heating to 80 degreesc , and 1.5 g of calcium hydroxide was added , then 2 ml of 4 - chlorobenzoylchloride was added dropwise within 20 min .
after refluxing for 2 h , the reaction mixture was poured into 30 ml diluted hydrochloric acid ( 3 n ) .
a cream colored powder was obtained by filtration , and used for recrystallization from ethanol to give colorless needle crystals .
